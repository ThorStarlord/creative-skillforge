# Skill: Story Revision

This skill provides a layered, bottom-up diagnosis of existing text to identify root causes of narrative weakness while respecting authorial intent.

## How to Use
1.  Load the **Story Revision Meta-Prompt v2**.
2.  Provide the **TEXT** to be revised and any **CONTEXT** or **ACCEPTED DECISIONS**.
3.  Define the **SCOPE** (SCENE, CHAPTER, ARC, or FULL STORY).
4.  Review the diagnosis following the **DIAGNOSTIC ORDER**:
    1.  Principles
    2.  Containers
    3.  Representation
    4.  Modulators
    5.  Delivery

## Best Practices
*   Stop at the first layer of failure. Fix the core principles before worrying about POV or pacing.
*   Use "Ask-Then-Analyze" mode for dense or ambiguous scenes.
*   Preserve intentional ambiguity and subtext.
