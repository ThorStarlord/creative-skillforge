# Skill: Story Revision

This skill provides a layered, bottom-up diagnosis of existing text to identify root causes of narrative weakness by tracing symptoms back to their structural origins.

## How to Use
1.  Load the **Story Revision Meta-Prompt v3**.
2.  Provide the **TEXT** to be revised and any **CONTEXT** or **ACCEPTED DECISIONS**.
3.  Define the **SCOPE** (SCENE, CHAPTER, ARC, or FULL STORY).
4.  Review the diagnosis following the **DIAGNOSTIC ORDER (Bottom-Up)**:
    1.  **Identify the Symptom** (Usually in Modulation or Representation).
    2.  **Trace Downward**: Check Modulation -> Representation -> Carriers -> Threads -> Structural Forces -> Scope -> Promise -> Target Experience.

## Best Practices
*   Stop at the first layer of failure. Fix the core structural forces before worrying about POV or pacing.
*   Use "Ask-Then-Analyze" mode for dense or ambiguous scenes.
*   Ensure the fix aligns with the intended **Target Experience**.
