# Skill: Story Ideation

This skill uses a top-down approach to build a bidirectional story engine, starting from the target experience and moving down to representation and modulation.

## How to Use
1.  Load the **Story Ideation Meta-Prompt v4**.
2.  Provide any **CONTEXT** or **SEED IDEAS** you have.
3.  Define the **TARGET EXPERIENCE** (or ask the AI to propose options).
4.  Follow the **CORE GENERATION ORDER**:
    *   **Layer 1**: Target Experience
    *   **Layer 2**: Promise / Constraints
    *   **Layer 3**: Scope / Scale
    *   **Layer 4**: Structural Forces
    *   **Layer 5**: Threads / Modules
    *   **Layer 6**: Carriers
    *   **Layer 7**: Representation
    *   **Layer 8**: Modulation
    *   **Layer 9**: Resonance / Coherence Check

## Best Practices
*   Don't skip layers. Ensure the Target Experience is solid before choosing a Genre or Scope.
*   **Character detail is expressive (Carriers); agency is structural (Structural Forces).**
*   Treat AI suggestions as hypotheses.
*   Focus on **Pressure and Contradiction** to create strong structural engines.
