# Skill: Story Ideation

This skill uses a top-down approach to build a story engine, starting from the emotional target and moving down to structural principles and scene design.

## How to Use
1.  Load the **Story Ideation Meta-Prompt v3**.
2.  Provide any **CONTEXT** or **SEED IDEAS** you have.
3.  Define the **TARGET FEELING** (or ask the AI to propose options).
4.  Follow the **CORE GENERATION ORDER**:
    *   **Layer 1**: Target Feeling
    *   **Layer 2**: Genre / Mode
    *   **Layer 3**: Structural Principles
    *   **Layer 4**: Mechanics / Systems
    *   **Layer 5**: Scenes / Execution

## Best Practices
*   Don't skip layers. Ensure the Feeling is solid before choosing a Genre.
*   Treat AI suggestions as hypotheses.
*   Focus on **Pressure and Contradiction** to create strong structural engines.
